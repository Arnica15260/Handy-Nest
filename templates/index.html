Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, we answered with care.

Do you have a question before getting started?

We’ve put together answers to the most common things people ask us—so you can feel confident and informed before using Handy Nest.

What services does Handy Nest provide?

We connect you with qualified teachers, caring nurses, and dedicated volunteers to meet your educational, healthcare, and community support needs.

How can I request a teacher, nurse, or volunteer from Handy Nest?

Simply contact us through our website or customer support, and we’ll match you with the right professional for your needs.

Are the teachers and nurses experienced?

Yes, all of our team members are highly trained, certified, and experienced professionals dedicated to providing top-quality service.

Is there a fee for the services provided?

Yes, there is a service fee for the professionals we connect you with. The exact fee depends on the specific service required, and we’ll provide a clear breakdown when you make a request.

How do you ensure the safety of your volunteers and professionals?

All our professionals undergo thorough background checks and are trained to adhere to the highest standards of safety and professionalism.
